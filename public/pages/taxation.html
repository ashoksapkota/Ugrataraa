Individual Income Tax (FY 2082/83)

Income tax in Nepal operates on a progressive slab system. A 1% Social Security Tax applies to the base slab for individuals not enrolled in the SSF.

Unmarried Individuals

  • Up to NPR 500,000 1%
  • NPR 500,001 – 700,000 10%
  • NPR 700,001 – 1,000,000 20%
  • NPR 1,000,001 – 2,000,000 30%
  • Above NPR 2,000,000 36% - 39%

Married Couples

  • Up to NPR 600,000 1%
  • NPR 600,001 – 800,000 10%
  • NPR 800,001 – 1,100,000 20%
  • NPR 1,100,001 – 2,000,000 30%
  • Above NPR 2,000,000 36% - 39%

Value Added Tax (VAT)

The standard VAT rate is 13%. Registration is mandatory if business transactions in the last 12 months exceed specific thresholds.

  • Goods Threshold: Exceeding NPR 50 Lakhs (5 Million).
  • Services Threshold: Exceeding NPR 20 Lakhs (2 Million).
  • Filing Deadline: By the 25th of the following Nepali month.
